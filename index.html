Our Donate Plan

Recently different districts of our country
especially Sylhet, Kurigram have been affected by floods.
We will go to those places with help and cooperate as much as possible.

Food for families

The first thing most families need is food. So our first goal is to deliver food to the families

Post-flood rehabilitation

It is necessary to rebuild the houses of the families after recovering from the danger of floods.So we will continue the rehabilitation work in a few steps

Medicine for the sick

Our next task is to provide emergency medicine to the flood-injured and sick people which is very important for them in the post-flood period.
